Born at ODTÜ,
Rooted in Antakya

Founded within the innovation ecosystem of Middle East Technical University (ODTÜ), Antioch Labs merges academic rigor with cultural depth.

Antioch (Antakya) has been a crossroads of civilizations for millennia—a living mosaic where diverse cultures merge to create something stronger than the sum of their parts.

We carry this heritage into the digital age. We believe that software, like a city, should bring people together. We design robust architectures for communities and organizations, drawing strength from our technical excellence and our cultural capability to harmonize differences.
